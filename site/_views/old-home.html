Evolve

Our approach is different. meta5’s infrastructure combines your strategic data and tools, providing you with a platform to rapidly respond to ever changing market conditions. We value success by how long it takes to answer a new business question. By doing so, meta5 evolves with you.

process

1.

rapid response time

meta5 easily manipulates  any kind of data from anywhere, so you spendless time managing it and more time making strategic decisions from it.
2.

automates manual effort

meta5 automates the time-consuming transformation of data needed for reporting and distribution. With meta5, your Data Analysts can get the job done in a fraction of the time (and get back to what you hired them to do, analyze data). We do this all without the need to code (that’s right, we’re code-free).
3.

reporting governance

meta5 audits, manages, and documents the data and report creation process so you understand exactly where your numbers came from and can reproduce them anytime you need.
4.

scales with you

Unlike other BI tools, meta5’s solutions adapts and scales with you. Have a new data source? No problem. meta5 dynamically adopts this source so you can rapidly respond. Need a new type of output? No sweat.meta5 adapts to integrate any specialized program needed.
